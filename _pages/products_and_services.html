In the complex times we live in, we assist you and your organisation in saving lives thanks to a better match between humans and technology.

Ever heard of large failures like those of 112, OVH, Amazon, ...? Shit happens, that's why our approach is resilience-focused. To this end, we propose both tools and advices.

For instance, Poppy Alert will notify your personnel and partners in case of emergency. Be it an Hospital Emergency Plan, Medical Intervention Plan or General Intervention plan, we have a simple but sharp solution for you: just push on a button, and that's it. Just the necessary amount of automation, but no more, as it is intended to work when the rest have failed, and in particular more complex systems that are integrated in your daily business processes.

ColMa helps you to seamlessly collaborate on a map, from the first minute you have it in your hands. No need for training, and if it fails, just resorts to paper maps as before: it is just the same, after all.

Finally, our Consulting services help you to evolve towards a more resilient organization in an information technology driven environment.
We'll help you with just what is needed: be it an analysis, a participatory process, advices to be more resilient, a piece of software or related services (like maps or a geographical information system), competencies for a better use of information systems in emergency planning and crisis management, or all that at once.
